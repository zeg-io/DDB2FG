Instructions

  1. Create D&D5E Character on D&D Beyond (Characters from Taking20 work as well).
  2. Export Character you just created to .PDF and save to your computer
  3. Drag PDF to box above that says 'Drag & Drop Files' or click 'Upload' and nagivate to where you saved the PDF.
  4. XML will be populated in 'XML Text Area'
  5. Copy/paste text into new document, give it an extention of .xml instead of .txt
  6. Import .xml file into PC/Characters ribbon in Fantasy Grounds
